About Us

HJ Magnolia is dedicated to providing compassionate, culturally competent care tailored to the needs of our diverse community. Our mission is to support mental wellness by creating a healing space where individuals and families can thrive.

We serve children and families through a variety of therapeutic and support services designed to meet the unique needs of each child. Our goal is to foster emotional growth, resilience, and well-being in a safe and nurturing environment.

Our Services

  • Individual Therapy
  • Group Therapy
  • Medication Management
  • Psychiatric Evaluations
  • Case Management
  • Trauma-Informed Care
